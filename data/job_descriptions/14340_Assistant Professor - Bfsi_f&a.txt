The field of Quantitative Finance is relatively new in India and IIQF is the first institute of its kind in India that is exclusively dedicated to this extremely specialized field. The objective of IIQF is to bring to the academic domain in India the technologies and skill-sets that are used by the practitioners at the highest level in the finance industry. The idea is to bridge the considerable gap that exists between what the industry demands and what the academia produces. IIQF conducts various courses for individuals and training programs for corporate in areas of Quantitative Finance and allied disciplines where multi-disciplinary skills are essential. The courses it offers involve interdisciplinary course of study in Investment Finance, Mathematics, Statistics, Stochastics, Numerical Computations, Programming, etc. The Institute provides high-end training courses for professionals working in, or intending to take up a career in Quantitative Investment Management, Derivatives Trading, Funds Management, Algorithmic Trading, Financial Risk Management, Structured Finance, Portfolio Management, Treasury, Financial Consulting Services, Financial Software & Systems, Securities Trading, etc. Faced with an ever-increasing range of generalized finance and MBA courses, the courses at IIQF are unique in its structured approach and commitment to the practical application of Quantitative Finance in the field of finance. What differentiates the courses at IIQF is the emphasis on the practical applications of the subjects and theories rather than just the study of the theories alone.
