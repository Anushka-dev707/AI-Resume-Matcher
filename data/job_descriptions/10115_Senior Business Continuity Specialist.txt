TITLE: Senior Business Continuity Specialist

SKILLS: nan

Job Description Â Send me Jobs like this JOB DESCRIPTION The Senior BC Specialist supports the Business Continuity and Crisis Management program through the programs business continuity life cycle. Monitors current and emerging hazards, threats, and risks, and assesses the effect and potential impact to the business. Implements the emergency notification system, extracts relevant data and information from internal and external resources, and responds to audits and RFP. Responds to crisis management and leads efforts during response, recovery, and resumption phases, and facilitates business continuity and crisis management strategies across all business lines. RESPONSIBILITIES Maintain global and standard operating procedures for site Business Continuity plans that are complementary to and supportive of the business unit continuity planning process. Provides monthly updates of the status of BRM and BC plan readiness. Assist in developing and maintaining guidelines, support documents and trainings to ensure an efficient, effective, and integrated worldwide organization for emergency management and business resumption. Assess and provide key data to create a sites crisis management playbook. Utilizes company programs/systems to extract key information to determine the business impact (CTMS, PeopleSoft, SWN) Ensure business continuity plans are complete and aligned with corporate standards for all sites. Develop and deliver business continuity communications that provide essential communications related to activity where normal communication channels may not be available. Identify processes and products to eliminate or mitigate identified BC/DR risks. Anticipate changing customer requirements and proactively act upon them in a timely manner. Ensure KPI reflect a continued state of readiness for the Global Business Continuity Program. Maintain good communications and working relationships with Disaster Recovery Teams and external clients. Document business continuity/incident management documentation, report findings and lesson learns. Identify best practices and Business Continuity Planning tools and resources. Provide timely responses to RFP and RFI requests, and provide services as needed. Coordinate the execution of global crisis management team and recovery efforts. Provides research data that identifies risks and risk mitigation for new business. Facilitate business continuity presentations for local and global Customer audits. Facilitate emergency response efforts to include fire evacuations, medical responders and inclement weather closures. Ensure the emergency notification system is tested and trains designated administrators. Performs other duties as directed All responsibilities are essential job functions unless noted as nonessential (N). REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES Professional certification(s) in business continuity, systems administration or project management. Possess strong organizational, written and verbal communication, and technical skills. Possess advanced working knowledge of the Business Continuity planning life cycle Possess understanding of risk management, physical and logical security concepts, such as NFPA 1600, ISO 27001, and ISO 31000. Understand Emergency Preparedness and Crisis Management strategies. Ability to manage ongoing projects and tasks independently. Excellent communication (both verbal and written), presentation, and interpersonal skills. Ability to manage crisis communications and work in a crisis management operations center Self-starter and highly motivated. Experience working with key management and maintains effective working relationships with clients. Managerial experience, which includes leadership, issue management, team building, and coaching. High level of attention to detail and accuracy. Proficient with Microsoft Office applications, emergency notification systems, and BC software MINIMUM REQUIRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE Bachelors degree with 4 years business resumption planning/administration experience including 1 year business resumption planning/administration experience in a global or international companys BC organization; or equivalent combination of education, training and experience . Certification in Business Continuity Planning PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS Position may require a significant amount of writing and keyboarding involving repetitive motions with fingers and sitting for prolonged periods of time. Position requires extensive use of the telephone and face-to-face communications, which requires the accurate perception of speech. Minimal travel is required.
