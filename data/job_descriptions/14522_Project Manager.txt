Job Description Â Send me Jobs like this A person responsible for timely implementation of new projects. Broadly, a Project includes a set of parts for which samples that are required to be developed and subsequently converted into series production and sales. The Project Managers Prime Responsibility is to wrap his arms around the Project and carry it forward in order to ensure its implementation as per the Plan. It is expected that he should be updated on the latest Status of the Project at any given point of time Chalk out a Time line Plan for various activities identified under Project Management in order to meet the Project Implementation date specified by the Customer. Maintain a close follow-up with various functions in order to make sure that the Project is running as per the Plan Trouble shooting in case one function is not able to adhere to their time line for want of an input from any other function. It is the Job of the Project Manager to arrange the desired Input from the former and provide it to the latter. Needs to collect and preserve the entire Dossier for his Project, like APQP discussions, Minutes Of Meeting, ISIR Reports & PPAP Documents, and any other information acquired / experiences during the product development process. Required to hand over this Dossier to the Production department once the samples are approved.
